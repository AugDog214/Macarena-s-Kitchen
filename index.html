Welcome to Macarena's Kitchen — Cape Coral's spot for making sure flavors around the world American fusion is done right! From our signature Philly Cheesesteaks to homemade pasta, fresh Saturday breakfast, and everything in between — every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and love.
