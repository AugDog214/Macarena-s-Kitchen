ABOUT US

For us, food has always meant more than just a meal — it's how we connect, how we celebrate, and how we come together at the end of the day. The kitchen has always been the center of our home, led by a mom who loves to cook a variety of dishes made with care, flavor, and love.

Our family runs this kitchen together. Our chef brings years of experience in Italian cuisine, blending tradition, technique, and passion into every plate. Our kids are right there with us — learning, helping, and growing — because this isn't just a business, it's our way of life.

We believe in hard work, unity, and giving back to the community that supports us. That's why Macarena's Kitchen is built on family values: respect, togetherness, and gratitude. When tasting our food — we want you to feel like part of that family.

Every dish is prepared with intention and whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, our hope is that you taste the love, feel the warmth, and always choose Macarena's as a place where food brings people together.

Proudly located inside the Fraternal Order of Eagles #3957 on SE 9th Place in Cape Coral, FL. Dine in, pick up, or order delivery straight to your door.
